We are investing in access and digital growth of regions. We do it with a sense of progress, human sense, environmental sense, ethical sense and sense of belonging with Colombia.
People want to bring services and content even if they change device, for that reason, we defend portability and interoperability of digital life of services and applications. An open digital life means: open domain names, open Internet access, accelerate innovation through cooperation, seize the opportunity presented by open data and redefine education to democratize access to it by the internet.
